Moscow sites

VDNKH

VDNKH (short for what can be translted as "Peoples achievments exibition") is a huge park with different pavillions, and different museums, as well as wonderful fountains and monuments. It has being resurged and improved since 2014, and became a great point of attraction for Moscowians and city guests.

Seven sisters

There are seven "Stalin towers" built in 1950s. They all look alike for a stranger, although for me they all are very different. One (the most beautiful one, of course), for example, is the main building of my university. Another one is the building of Ministry of forreign affairs. Some of them are hotels or living buildings. By the way, they have an eighth sister in Warsaw, which is also quite beautiful, although not so majestic.
